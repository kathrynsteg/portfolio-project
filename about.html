Hello, I'm Kathryn Stegeman

Front-end Developer, based in Chicago

Pairing my strengths in problem-solving and resiliently adapting to complex challenges with the robust knowledge and experience I gained from the SheCodes Front-End Development bootcamp, I decided to pivot my career towards software development. This transition represents not just a career change, but a natural evolution of my lifelong passion for learning and innovation.
